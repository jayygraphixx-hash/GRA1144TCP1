Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo is one of those comfort dishes that never really goes out of style. It’s rich, creamy, and filling—perfect for a cozy dinner or when you want something that feels a little indulgent. At its core, it’s pretty simple: tender pieces of chicken served over fettuccine and coated in a smooth Alfredo sauce made from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. Even though the ingredients are basic, the flavors come together in a way that feels luxurious.

ingredients

  • Fettuccine - uncooked
  • Chicken – boneless skinless
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Olive Oil
  • Cream Cheese
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan cheese – shredded
  • Parsley – fresh

    instructions

      Make the noodles: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine and cook until al dente according to package directions, usually 10 minutes. Reserve 1/2 cup of the cooking water, then drain well. Set aside.
      Make the chicken: Season chicken breasts with the Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
      Warm the olive oil over medium-high heat in a large nonstick skillet. Once it’s shimmering, swirl the pan to evenly distribute. Add the chicken and leave it undisturbed for 5-7 minutes,
      Make the Alfredo sauce: In the same pan, over medium-low heat, add the butter and cream; whisk until butter has melted.
